Program Hero International School of Beauty Therapy was originally established in 1971... The Australasia International School (AIS) was previously known as the International School of Beauty Therapy owned by Onyx Developments Pty Ltd . It was established in 1973 in Freemantle, Western Australia. In 2017 Onyx Developments Pty Ltd under new ownership moved to Sydney and was rebranded as Australasia International School (AIS). AIS prides itself in being able to deliver qualifications that prepare students for the workforce in the areas of beauty therapies, massage therapies and management. Students enrolled in the Beauty or Massage Therapies become experts in both theory and practice. AIS has a working clinic that is open to the general public where students work in a genuine business environment. Their experience of providing beauty and massage treatments, guided by industry professionals, is a fundamental aspect of their development. Students undertaking management studies in the areas of Project Management and Leadership & Management have the advantage of trainers who share their considerable skills and expertise. The Australasia International students enjoy our well-resourced CBD campus and its access to all the convenience the city of Sydney has to offer.
